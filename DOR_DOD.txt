defenation of done: - 
1) finalaize all the requirments
2) pass all the test
3) integrated succesfully



defenation of ready: -
Having a Definition of Ready means that stories must be immediately actionable. The Team must be able to determine
what needs to be done and the amount of work required to complete the User Story or Product Backlog Item

important for DOR:-

The user, their needs, motivations and frustrations.
The user’s context and what they are trying to achieve, and how the user will get value from this – and what is either side of this story.
The context of the system(s) in this part of the journey and relevant to this item.
The business logic needed to accomplish this.
